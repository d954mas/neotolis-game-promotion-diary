## Decisions Made

- **Pre-emptive 0005+0006 split:** drizzle-kit emitted a single combined migration; we manually forked it. Driven by Pitfall 1 (Postgres 16 transaction rules) + Plan 02.1-12 precedent.
- **userId denormalized on event_games:** required for the ESLint tenant-scope rule to have a column to walk for (rule cannot inspect FK-chained values).
- **events.gameId column DROPPED rather than retained as cache:** drift risk too high; pre-launch makes destructive cheap; Plan 02.1-28 migrates consumers in lock-step.
- **game_steam_listings unique stays UNCONDITIONAL:** Plan 02.1-29 chose Path B (service-layer pre-INSERT lookup) so we leave the constraint as-is; only the user-scoped uniqueness is DROPPED.
- **New audit verb event.detached_from_game (not reuse of attached_to_game):** symmetric inverse with distinct semantics; AuditRow / FilterChips switch cases in Plan 02.1-28 render distinct labels.
- **Stale Plan 02.1-01 test marked it.skip rather than deleted:** preserves the Wave 0 trail; superseded annotation points readers at Plan 02.1-27.
- **Plan 02.1-24 hard-length assertion relaxed to lower bound:** the exact-length assertion now lives in Plan 02.1-27's describe block, so future additive enum migrations don't double-fail at every prior plan.
