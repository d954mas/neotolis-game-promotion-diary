## Decisions Made

- **`POSTGRES_PASSWORD` documented as a Rule 2 auto-add deviation.** docker-compose.prod.yml interpolates `${POSTGRES_PASSWORD}` into both the postgres service environment AND the app/worker/scheduler `DATABASE_URL`, but the variable was missing from `.env.example`. Operators following install.md §4 step 4 ("Fill values:") would have been told to set a var they couldn't find in the template. Critical for correct/secure operation. Added with a clear comment about local-dev (uses postgres/postgres default) vs production (must be generated via `openssl rand -base64 32`).
- **Russian UAT lives in §6 of install.md** rather than as a separate file. The operator consults the UAT script after every deploy (per VALIDATION.md "Manual-Only Verifications" → "End-to-end Russian UAT"); coupling it to the runbook means one bookmark for both "how to deploy" and "how to verify the deploy is good".
- **5 rclone provider flows** documented (R2 / B2 / Wasabi / AWS / MinIO) plus local-only escape hatch — matches the self-host parity invariant (any operator can pick any backend or none) while making R2 the recommended path for the SaaS author.
- **12 RESEARCH "Common Pitfalls" → 12 install.md §7 FAQ headings** with one inline mention of Pitfall 11 ("Build-time env placeholders leak") because the mitigation is "no operator action needed; verified once in Phase 1 review" — keeping the FAQ entry short honors AGENTS.md's "comments only WHY a future reader cannot derive" rule.
