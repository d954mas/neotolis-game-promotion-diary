## Decisions Made

- **Option-1 (inline extension) over option-2 (separate steps)** for the SaaS-leak grep additions — keeps the 4 related patterns under one step name. Renamed the step from `SaaS-leak grep (D-14)` to `SaaS-leak grep (D-14, D-30)`.
- **Whitelist regex uses path-prefix anchors with explicit `:` at end of env.ts entry** — `^src/lib/server/config/env\.ts:` (note the trailing colon) so that future files like `env-loader.ts` are NOT accidentally whitelisted. - **Cache strategy: GHA store** (`type=gha,mode=max`) — no external cache backend (Docker Hub free tier has bandwidth limits; S3 would cost). GHA store is free for public repos and scoped per-branch; meets the indie-budget constraint.
